Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of forward-thinking developers and has grown into one of the most respected technology conferences globally. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of professionals, thought leaders, and groundbreaking innovators.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, encourage collaboration, and empower the next generation of technology leaders. We believe in accessible, inclusive, and sustainable tech solutions.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Rivera

Dr. Ada Rivera is a pioneer in quantum computing and an advocate for diversity in STEM. Her keynote in 2022 inspired many to explore advanced computing frontiers.

James Li

James Li, a leader in AI ethics, shared insights on building responsible machine learning systems. His 2023 session remains one of the highest-rated in TechCon history.
